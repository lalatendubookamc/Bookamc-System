# BOOKAMC Compliance Calculator - Business Goals

## Goal 1: Lead Generation

### Objective
Capture and identify high-potential enterprise prospects through compliance self-assessment.

### Key Metrics
- Number of completed assessments
- Assessment completion rate
- Lead qualification score
- Contact information capture rate

### Implementation Strategy
- Free/freemium calculator access
- Minimal required fields at entry
- Progressive profiling through assessment
- Lead scoring based on assessment results
- Automated lead routing to sales team

### Success Criteria
- 1000+ assessments in first 6 months
- 25%+ conversion to qualified leads
- Average lead score >60/100

---

## Goal 2: Compliance Assessment

### Objective
Provide accurate, comprehensive evaluation of organizational regulatory readiness.

### Key Metrics
- Assessment accuracy vs. external audits
- Dimension coverage (6+ areas)
- Benchmark comparisons
- Assessment reliability score

### Implementation Strategy
- Multi-dimensional assessment questionnaire
- Industry-specific variants
- Automated gap analysis
- Benchmarking against industry standards
- Detailed assessment reports

### Success Criteria
- >90% accuracy vs. professional audits
- 6+ assessment dimensions
- Benchmark data for 5+ industries
- Assessment completion in <30 minutes

---

## Goal 3: Risk Scoring

### Objective
Identify and quantify critical compliance gaps and operational risks.

### Key Metrics
- Risk score accuracy
- Gap identification rate
- Risk prediction accuracy
- Remediation success rate

### Implementation Strategy
- Multi-factor risk model
- Weighted scoring algorithm
- Risk categorization (regulatory, operational, security, reputational)
- Priority-based gap ranking
- Risk trend analysis

### Success Criteria
- Risk scores correlate with actual incidents
- >80% of predicted gaps confirmed in audits
- Clear risk prioritization
- Actionable remediation paths

---

## Goal 4: AMC Recommendation

### Objective
Convert compliance assessment results into targeted AMC service contracts.

### Key Metrics
- Recommendation relevance score
- Service adoption rate
- Contract closure rate
- Average contract value

### Implementation Strategy
- Gap-to-service mapping
- Customized service packages
- ROI calculations
- Implementation roadmaps
- Service bundling recommendations

### Success Criteria
- Relevant recommendations to 95%+ of assessments
- 30%+ of recommendations convert to contracts
- Average contract value >$50K
- Service adoption within 3 months of recommendation

---

## Goal 5: Sales Qualification

### Objective
Prioritize high-value leads and optimize sales team efficiency.

### Key Metrics
- Lead quality score
- Sales conversion rate
- Sales cycle reduction
- Win rate improvement
- Average deal size

### Implementation Strategy
- Automated lead scoring
- Qualification criteria (company size, industry, risk level)
- Lead segmentation by value tier
- Sales readiness signals
- Integration with CRM

### Success Criteria
- Sales cycle reduced by 40%
- Win rate increased by 35%
- Average deal size >$75K
- 50%+ of leads are sales-qualified
- Improved sales team productivity

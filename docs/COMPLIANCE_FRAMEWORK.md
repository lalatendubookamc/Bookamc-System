# BOOKAMC Compliance Framework

## Overview
The BOOKAMC Compliance Framework is designed to assess organizational compliance against NBCS 2026 and BIS standards.

## Framework Components

### 1. Regulatory Assessment Module
- Evaluate current compliance status
- Map requirements across regulations
- Identify compliance gaps
- Track remediation efforts

### 2. Risk Identification Module
- Categorize regulatory risks
- Assess impact and probability
- Generate risk scores
- Prioritize mitigation strategies

### 3. Compliance Scoring System
- Multi-dimensional assessment
- Weighted scoring methodology
- Comparative benchmarking
- Progress tracking

### 4. Recommendation Engine
- Suggest AMC services
- Prioritize remediation actions
- Generate compliance roadmap
- Estimate implementation timeline

## Compliance Standards Covered

### NBCS 2026 (National Business Compliance Standards)
- Data Protection Requirements
- Operational Standards
- Governance Frameworks
- Audit Requirements

### BIS Standards (Bureau of Indian Standards)
- Quality Management Systems
- Information Security
- Environmental Compliance
- Business Continuity

## Assessment Dimensions

1. **Governance** - Leadership, policies, oversight
2. **Risk Management** - Identification, assessment, mitigation
3. **Compliance** - Regulatory requirements, monitoring, reporting
4. **Security** - Data protection, access controls, encryption
5. **Operations** - Process efficiency, resource allocation, training
6. **Technology** - Systems, tools, infrastructure readiness

## Scoring Methodology

- **Score Range**: 0-100
- **Maturity Levels**: 
  - 0-20: Initial/Ad-hoc
  - 21-40: Developing
  - 41-60: Managed
  - 61-80: Optimized
  - 81-100: Advanced

## Compliance Roadmap

Once assessment is complete, the calculator generates:
- Priority action items
- Resource requirements
- Timeline estimates
- Cost/benefit analysis
- AMC service recommendations
